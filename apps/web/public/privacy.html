Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Don Sosa Coach is a League of Legends coaching and analytics tool that lets players request analysis of their own match history using Riot ID data they provide.

Data We Process

  • Riot ID information entered by the user, such as game name and tagline.
  • Match history, timeline, rank, profile icon and other gameplay information returned by Riot APIs.
  • Derived analytics generated from those matches, such as champion pool, runes, matchups and coaching insights.

How We Use Data

  • To analyze a player’s own League of Legends matches and show coaching-oriented insights.
  • To improve the product, including aggregated benchmark models built from stored match snapshots.
  • To operate a private beta of the service.

Storage

User-entered Riot IDs, fetched match data and derived analytics may be stored on the server and in the user’s browser cache to improve performance, support refreshes and preserve historical analysis.

Sharing

We do not sell player data. Data is processed through Riot’s APIs and may be stored by Don Sosa Coach for product functionality, analytics and benchmarking.
