Don Sosa Coach

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-06

Don Sosa Coach is a League of Legends coaching and analytics product that processes Riot ID information and gameplay data only to provide player-facing review, coaching, benchmarking and improvement workflows.

Data We Process

  • Riot ID data submitted by the user, such as game name and tagline.
  • Gameplay data returned by Riot APIs, including profile, match history, timelines, ranks, champions, runes, builds and related analytics inputs.
  • Product account data needed to operate memberships, authentication, support, saved profiles and coaching workspace functionality.
  • Billing metadata handled through Stripe for subscriptions and account management. Full card data is processed by Stripe, not stored by Don Sosa Coach.

How We Use Data

  • To analyze matches, generate coaching insights and personalize the product experience.
  • To operate memberships, customer support, account security and coach-client workflows.
  • To improve the product through aggregated and de-identified benchmarking, quality checks and internal product analytics.

Retention and Deletion

User-submitted Riot IDs, fetched match snapshots and derived analytics may be stored while the account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to support continuity, renewals, support and fraud prevention. You can request deletion of account-linked data by contacting juliaansosa02@gmail.com.

Third Parties

Don Sosa Coach depends on Riot Games APIs, hosting providers, analytics infrastructure and Stripe for subscription billing. Each provider processes data as needed for the service to function.

Controller Contact

Don Sosa Coach operates this product from Argentina. For privacy, billing or deletion requests, contact juliaansosa02@gmail.com.
