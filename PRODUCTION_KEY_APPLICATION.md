## One-line product description

Don Sosa Coach is a League of Legends coaching and performance review web app that lets players analyze their own match history and receive role-aware insights about champion pool, matchups, runes, early game consistency and improvement priorities.

## Suggested short application summary

Don Sosa Coach is a web-based League of Legends coaching tool focused on post-game self-review. A player enters their Riot ID, the application fetches their recent matches through Riot APIs, and the product generates role-aware analytics and coaching insights across champion pool, matchups, runes, economy, consistency and recent trends. The goal is to help players understand what is holding them back and what to focus on in their next games.

## Suggested use case text

This product is a player-facing training and analytics tool. It is intended for players who want to review their own match history and receive structured coaching-style feedback. The app does not provide hidden information during live games and does not dictate in-game decisions from unknown real-time data. It is focused on self-review, pattern detection and improvement planning after matches.

## Suggested user flow text

1. A player opens the website.
2. The player enters their Riot Game Name and Tag Line.
3. The app downloads recent matches and timelines for that player.
4. The app shows coaching-oriented analysis, including priorities, champion trends, rune usage, matchup performance and recent match review.
5. The player uses that information to study their own performance and choose what to work on next.

## Suggested “why production key” text

The current prototype is fully functional and publicly accessible. We are applying for a production key because development keys expire too quickly and are not appropriate for a stable player-facing website. We need a production key to support a real hosted experience, maintain reliable access for users, and continue improving the tool as a legitimate coaching product.

## Suggested “what data do you store” text

We store the Riot ID provided by the user, match history and derived analytics that are needed to display coaching insights, preserve analysis state and improve the product. We do not expose the Riot API key to end users. We do not sell player data.

## Suggested “why should this be approved” text

The app is already live as a working prototype on a public domain, includes public Terms of Service and Privacy Policy pages, and demonstrates a clear user flow from Riot ID input to post-game coaching output. The use case is a training and analytics product for players reviewing their own gameplay, which aligns with Riot’s documented approved categories for player stats and training tools.
